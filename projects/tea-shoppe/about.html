About Us

Lotus Blossom Tea Shoppe was started by Bronx natives who had a desire to bring the tea shoppe experience to the South Bronx. Located in Parkchester neighborhood, this quaint tea shoppe serves a wide variety of herbal teas in a soothing, intimate atmosphere.

Our selection of teas changes daily, but we have a few staples which are usually in stock. These include:

— Chamomile

— Spearmint

— Peppermint

— Raspberry Mint

— Green

— English Breakfast

If there is a particular tea you absolutely must have, we recommend calling ahead to make sure that tea is in stock that day.
